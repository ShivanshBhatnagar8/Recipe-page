Pesto Pasta Salad

Pesto Pasta Salad is a refreshing and flavorful dish that's perfect for a summer picnic or a quick weeknight meal. This recipe typically features cooked pasta tossed with a homemade pesto sauce made from fresh basil, garlic, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil. The pasta and pesto are then mixed with cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and sliced black olives for added texture and flavor. This pasta salad can be served warm or cold and is always a crowd-pleaser.

Roasted Salmon with Herbed Butter

Roasted Salmon with Herb Butter is an elegant and healthy meal that's perfect for a special occasion or a dinner party. This recipe typically features a fresh salmon fillet coated in a flavorful blend of herbs, such as parsley, thyme, and rosemary, and roasted in the oven until tender and flaky. Caprese Salad

Caprese Salad is a simple and delicious Italian salad that's made from just a few fresh ingredients. This recipe typically features sliced ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, and whole basil leaves arranged on a plate or platter. The salad is then drizzled with olive oil and balsamic vinegar, and seasoned with salt and pepper. This salad is a great way to showcase the flavors of summer and is perfect as a light lunch or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pasta, such as fusilli or rotini
  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves, packed
  • 1/2 cup pine nuts
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
⨯

Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water. In a food processor, combine basil, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and garlic. Pulse until finely chopped. With the food processor running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the mixture is smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste. In a large bowl, toss the pasta with the pesto until well coated. Add the cherry tomatoes and toss to combine. Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pasta, such as fusilli or rotini
  • 4 salmon fillets
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh dill
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
⨯

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper. In a small bowl, mix together the butter, parsley, dill, chives, and lemon juice. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Top each fillet with a dollop of the herbed butter. Roast for 12-15 minutes, until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.

Ingredients

  • 2 large ripe tomatoes
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
⨯

Slice the tomatoes and arrange them on a large platter. Top the tomatoes with the sliced mozzarella cheese. Scatter the basil leaves over the cheese and tomatoes. In a small bowl, whisk together the balsamic vinegar and olive oil. Drizzle the dressing over the salad. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
